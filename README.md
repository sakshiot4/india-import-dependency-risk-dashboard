# 🇮🇳 India Import Dependency Risk Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing India's import dependency 
on China and other geopolitical rivals across key sectors 
from 2020 to 2024, with a Jan–Feb 2026 live snapshot.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages
- Page 1 — National Risk Overview
- Page 2 — Sector Deep Dive
- Page 3 — Country Concentration
- Page 4 — Trend Tracker

## 🗃️ Data Sources
- OEC World (oec.world)
- UN Comtrade (comtrade.un.org)
- GTRI Reports (gtri.in)
- ING Research / China Customs (Jan–Feb 2026 snapshot)

## 📁 Sectors Covered
Electronics, Pharma APIs, Plastics, Metals & Steel,
Instruments & Semiconductors, Textiles, 
Transportation, Stone & Glass

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

## 📅 Data Coverage
2020 – 2024 (full year) + Jan–Feb 2026 snapshot

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Electronics & Components is India's most China-dependent 
  sector at $70.88Bn in 2024
- Instruments & Semiconductors scored highest on composite 
  risk index
- China's share of India's total imports bounced back to 
  18% in 2023 after dipping to 13.9% in 2022
- YoY import growth hit 41% in 2021 — highest in 5 years
- Jan–Feb 2026 shows record $155B trade with +43.1% YoY growth
